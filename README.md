## 🧠 About Me

I'm a passionate **AI Solution Architect** focused on turning machine learning and deep learning into production-grade systems that solve real-world problems. I work across the full stack of modern AI — from **agentic, multi-agent networks** and **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)** to **model fine-tuning, evaluation, and deployment** — and I care as much about reliability and observability as I do about raw model capability.

- 🔭 Designing **agent-to-agent (A2A)** systems and LLM-powered platforms
- 🌱 Going deep on **fine-tuning, eval pipelines, and LLMOps**
- ⚡ Philosophy: *ship measurable, observable, reliable AI — not demos*
